      <div style="text-align: center; font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 10pt; font-weight: bold;">the Declaration of a Cash Dividend</div>

      <div style="text-align: center; font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 10pt; font-weight: bold;">Solid Start to the Year Led by Oil &amp; Gas Segment</div>
      <div>
        <div><br>
        </div>
      </div>
      <div>
        <hr noshade="noshade" align="center" style="height: 2px; color: #000000; background-color: #000000; text-align: center; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; border: none;"></div>
      <div style="text-align: justify; text-indent: 36pt; font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 10pt;"> <br>
      </div>
      <div style="text-align: justify; font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 10pt; text-indent: 36pt;">HONOLULU, HAWAII, February 10, 2023 -- Barnwell Industries, Inc. (NYSE American: BRN) today reported net earnings of $1,089,000, $0.11 per share,
        for the three months ended December 31, 2022, as compared to net earnings of $1,073,000, $0.11 per share, for the three months ended December 31, 2021.</div>
      <div style="text-indent: 36pt;">&#160;</div>
      <div style="text-align: justify; font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 10pt; text-indent: 36pt;"><font style="font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 10pt; color: rgb(0, 0, 0);">Mr. Alexander C. Kinzler, Chief Executive Officer of Barnwell,
          commented, &#8220;We are pleased to report that </font>our first quarter oil and gas segment <font style="font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 10pt; color: rgb(0, 0, 0);">revenues increased by over $1,300,000 (33%) as </font>prices increased
        for all products with oil, natural gas, and natural gas liquids increasing 6%, 4%, and 42%, respectively. Net oil production increased to 48,000 Bbls (barrels) from 39,000 Bbls (23%) and net natural gas production increased to 300,000 Mcf (1,000
        cubic feet) from 205,000 Mcf (46%), in the current quarter as compared to last year&#8217;s quarter, as we continue to refresh our oil and gas production through new drilling programs.</div>
      <div style="text-indent: 36pt;">&#160;</div>
      <div style="text-align: justify; color: rgb(0, 0, 0); font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 10pt; text-indent: 36pt;">&#8220;The increases in production were primarily due to additional working interests acquired and wells drilled in the Twining area
        in fiscal 2022 and were partially offset by a decrease in production from wells in Oklahoma. Barnwell&#8217;s oil and natural gas capital expenditures, including accrued capital expenditures and excluding additions and revisions to estimated asset
        retirement obligations (&#8220;Net Capital Expenditures&#8221;), totaled $5,928,000 for the three months ended December 31, 2022, as compared to $2,870,000 for the same period in the prior year. In the three months ended December 31, 2022, Canadian capital
        expenditures were primarily for the completion and equipping of wells and facilities at Twining and totaled $574,000. During fiscal 2022, B<font style="font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 10pt;">arnwell invested $11,052,000 in Net Capital
          Expenditures for oil and natural gas properties.&#8221;</font></div>
      <div>&#160;</div>
      <div style="clear: both; margin-top: 10pt; margin-bottom: 10pt;" class="BRPFPageBreakArea">
        <div style="text-align: center;" class="BRPFPageNumberArea"><font style="font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 8pt; font-weight: normal; font-style: normal;">1</font></div>
        <div style="page-break-after: always;" class="BRPFPageBreak">
          <hr noshade="noshade" style="border-width: 0px; clear: both; margin: 4px 0px; width: 100%; height: 2px; color: #000000; background-color: #000000;"></div>
      </div>
      <div style="text-align: justify; font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 10pt; text-indent: 36pt;"><font style="font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 10pt; color: rgb(0, 0, 0);">In the Twining area, Barnwell&#8217;s technical team has identified 16
          high quality, high working interests drilling locations on our controlled and operated lands.&#160; Success with these wells could translate to numerous additional follow-up locations on the same lands.&#160; Additionally, we have identified app</font>roximately

        10 additional drilling sites in North Twining Unit where we hold a 29% interest, four of which are forecasted to be drilled in the next 9 months.</div>
      <div style="text-indent: 36pt;">&#160;</div>
      <div style="text-align: justify; font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 10pt; text-indent: 36pt;">Our Oklahoma operations generated $517,000 (10%) of our oil and natural gas segment revenues for the three months ended December 31, 2022 and our
        oil and natural gas segment generated $1,986,000 of operating profit before general and administrative expenses in the three months ended December 31, 2022, an increase in operating results of $418,000 as compared to a $1,568,000 operating profit
        during the same period of the prior year.</div>
      <div style="text-indent: 36pt;">&#160;</div>
      <div style="text-align: justify; font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 10pt; text-indent: 36pt;">&#160;Our contract drilling segment s<font style="font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 10pt; color: rgb(0, 0, 0);">old one drilling rig and
          recognized a gain on the sale of $551,000 and </font>operating results increased $200,000 in the current period as compared to the prior year period.&#160; Our land investment segment saw the Kukio Resort Development Partnerships in which we own
        19.6% sell one lot within Increment I, resulting in the Company receiving $265,000 in percentage of sales payments and $478,000 in net cash distributions in the current period as compared to $600,000 in percentage of sales payments and $1,075,000
        in net cash distributions in the prior year period.</div>
      <div><br>
      </div>
      <div style="text-align: justify; font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 10pt; text-indent: 36pt;">General and administrative expenses increased $419,000 primarily due to increases in one-time, non-recurring professional fees in the current year
        period as compared to the same period in the prior year. The Company also incurred a $78,000 foreign currency gain in the current year period due to the effects of foreign exchange rate changes on intercompany advances as a result of the weakening
        of the U.S. dollar against the Canadian dollar in January.</div>
      <div><br>
      </div>
      <div style="text-align: justify; font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 10pt; text-indent: 36pt;">In January 2023, the Company entered into a cooperation and support agreement (the &#8220;Agreement&#8221;) among Mr. Kinzler<font style="font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 10pt; color: rgb(32, 32, 32);">, in his capacity as a major shareholder</font>, MRMP-Managers LLC, the Ned L. Sherwood Revocable Trust, NLS Advisory Group, Inc. and Ned L. Sherwood (collectively, the &#8220;MRMP Stockholders&#8221;),
        which we believe will be beneficial to the Company and all of our shareholders. The <font style="font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 10pt; color: rgb(32, 32, 32);">Agreement extends for two years the standstill terms of the previous
          agreement entered into with the MRMP Stockholders in 2021, ending the potential of a proxy contest at the 2023 annual meeting of stockholders. T</font>he expenses related to this agreement will be reflected in our results for our second quarter
        ending March 31, 2023.</div>
      <div><br>
      </div>
      <div style="text-align: justify; font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 10pt; text-indent: 36pt;">In December 2022, the Company, entered into a purchase and sale agreement with an independent third party to acquire a 22.3% non-operated working
        interest in oil and natural gas leasehold acreage in the Permian Basin in Texas for cash consideration of $806,000. In connection with the purchase of such leasehold interests, Barnwell acquired a 15.4% non-operated working interest in the planned
        drilling of two oil wells in the Wolfcamp Formation in Loving and Ward Counties, Texas and made a prepayment of $4,293,000 to pay its share of the estimated costs to drill, complete and equip the wells. In January 2023, the Company executed
        agreements to participate in the drilling of two more wells, at a 29% working interest, in our core property, the Twining area of Alberta, Canada, for approximately $1,500,000.</div>
      <div><br>
      </div>

      </div>
      <div style="text-align: justify; font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 10pt; text-indent: 36pt;">Barnwell ended the quarter with $6,747,000 in working capital, including $6,736,000 in cash and cash equivalents. All 3 of our business segments
        produced operating profits for the current quarter and the Board of Directors declared a cash dividend of $0.015 per share payable on March 13, 2023 to the holders of record as of the close of business on February 23, 2023.</div>
      <div style="text-indent: 36pt;">&#160;</div>
      <div style="text-align: justify; font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 10pt; text-indent: 36pt;">Mr. Kinzler also stated that &#8220;we are pleased to welcome Messrs. Joshua S. Horowitz and Laurance E. Narbut to our board. They were appointed to the
        Board of Directors of the Company on February 9, 2023. Also, on January 21, 2023, Mr. Kenneth S. Grossman was appointed Chairman of the Compensation Committee and Chairman of the Board of Directors and Mr. Douglas N. Woodrum was appointed Chairman
        of the Audit Committee and Nominating Committee of the Board of Directors. We look forward to the contributions of our new board members and our current board members in their new roles&#8221;.</div>
